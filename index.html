Color Palette

Color Palette is a website to create and download a palette of colors in the browser. Add as much colors as you want or delete them. With just a click all the colors and the hex values are downloaded to your computer. Thanks to David for the styling and the help. Click on the image and try it out

PRNT SCREENSHOT FINDER

The Website prnt.sc is a very popular website for sharing screenshots. Many people don't consider that the screenshots are avaiable for everyone through the generated link. With this website you can find screenshots by only a click. There are 3 sections Random, Link and Loop. Be careful some images contain nudity or scam. When you want to use the Loop function you have to allow pop-ups in your browser. Click on the image and try it out

WORDLE WORDS

The popular game Wordle is played by millions every day. Because I am not a native english speaker i often had quite some trouble to find a word matching the letters I allready figure out. So I built a website where you enter the letters you used and the website shows you all valid words and possible solutions. I usually start with ADIEU btw. Click on the image and try it out

NAME TO COLOR

Ever wonderd what your name would look like as a color? Now you have the chance to try it out. Just enter any text and click on GO. Your text will be convertet into a unique color and RGBA as well as the hex value will be shown. using the opacity range you can also ajust the opacity. Click on the image and try it out.
